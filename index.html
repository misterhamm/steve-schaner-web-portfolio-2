Hi there!

My name is Steve Schaner and I have taught middle and high school math since 2008. I enjoy helping others improve their lives. I also enjoy learning as much as I can to improve myself. I have always enjoyed anything to do with technology but didn't want to leave my students. My principal asked me to learn programming to teach it and I jumped on the opportunity. I learned as much as I could but the programming course dream didn't turn into a reality because of funding issues. It was too late. I was hooked. I've been learning web development ever since starting it in May 2017 and loving any chance I have to learn web development.

What Do I Provide?

I can provide you with a clean, responsive, and functional website. Whether your project is a simple one page site like this one or a larger project with many pages and functionality, I can help you market your business. Every project brings new and fun challenges to help your business grow and to help myself grow as a developer. Let's grow together.
